AlgoRhythm

AlgoRhythm is a Lindyhop and swing dance studio in Amsterdam Noord. We believe everyone can learn to dance and aim to create a safe and inclusive space where everyone can feel comfortable to do so. Find out more about us in our story.

One of the dances we teach is Lindy Hop, an improvisational partner dance to swinging jazz music. We like to focus on leading, following and connecting with your partner and the music.

Classes

In our weekly Lindyhop classes in Amsterdam Noord, we’ll teach you everything you need to know to get you out on the dance floor as soon as possible. Check out our schedule to find out when you can start.

AlgoRhythm is known for her inclusivity and collaborative atmosphere. In our classes pay a lot of attention to providing a good and safe learning environment for everyone, and encourage students to let go of traditional patterns such as gendered dance roles.

Events

Want to find out where you can dance all the moves you learn in class? Or perhaps you want to feel the atmosphere at a party first before you join our classes? There are plenty of opportunities for that in Amsterdam. Check out all the weekly swing dance parties!

Every August we organise a big Lindyhop workshop weekend: Ceuvel Swing Camp. A weekend full of Lindyhop group and private classes and parties with lots of dancing every night.
